    Welcome to Casadiellas Pastelería

    Enjoy delicious freshly baked pastries at ‘Casadiella Pastelería’ that are sure to delight your taste buds! Get ready to tantalize your palate with sweet confections made with love and care, baked from our oven delivered straight to your home. At ‘Casadiellas Pastelería’ we follow a fairly simple philosophy – offer a product of outstanding quality and ensure customer loyalty. The simplicity of that principle is backed up by a lot of hard work and years of experience. Casadiellas is specializing in freshly baked breads, Mousses, Pies and Dessert Bars. We also offer a variety of bite sized treats. Everything is made from scratch in house and with locally sourced ingredients when possible. Perfect as well to gift to friends, loved ones, or someone special for birthdays, Valentine's day, or any special day.

    About Us

    The "Casadiellas Pastelería" is a Spanish and French Brand that started as a small family business. The owners are Sophia Danielle Acosta and Warren Brimon, supported by a staff of 25 employees. Although not small any more, the business tries to keep the family atmosphere where we care not only about our company, our products and our staff, but also we consider each "customer" a member in this family of "casadiellas shoppers."
